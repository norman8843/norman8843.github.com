About Me

Research

Currently, I focus mostly on XML data management and sematic network. My research aims to integrate sematic network and traditional keyword search engine into a XML data management system. For more details on this please see my research page.

I am currently part of the XML data management group and Shanghai (International) Database Research Center at Fudan University. The group is led by Dr. Zhenying He and the Database Research Center is led by Prof. Wei Wang.
